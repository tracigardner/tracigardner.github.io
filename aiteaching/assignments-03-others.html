Outlines seven strategies for integrating AI into college teaching by assigning AI and students specific roles (e.g., Coach, Teammate, Tutor).

Assignment Collections

[table id=3 /]
AI assignment library (University of North Dakota)
A searchable collection of peer-reviewed, classroom-tested assignments for teaching with and about AI in higher education—useful across disciplines for integrating AI literacy.
Assignment Makeovers in the AI Age: Essay Edition by Derek Bruff (University of Virginia)
Shares examples of redesigned college writing assignments that address generative AI’s influence, helping instructors adapt essay tasks for authenticity and academic integrity.
Sample Activities, Teach@CUNY AI Toolkit
Provides adaptable classroom activities for teaching with AI, including prompts, discussions, and critical analysis tasks. Particularly useful for writing-intensive courses.
AI Workshop Prompts By José Antonio Bowen (Bowen Innovation Group L.L.C.)
Easy-to-use AI prompts from Teaching with AI: A Practical Guide to a New Era of Human Learning, copy and paste instead of retyping.
“AI Mini-Games for Peer Review” by Neeza Singh and Christopher McVey (Boston University)
A collection of short, interactive, low-stakes exercises to introduce generative AI concepts. Includes AI-Penned Dialogues, Character Depth Enhancer, Devil’s Advocate, Elevator Pitch, Sentence Surgery, and AI-Powered Mood Board.
ChatGPT Assignments to Use in Your Classroom Today (University of Central Florida)
Introduce core AI concepts, ethical issues, and real-world applications with activities from this open-access textbook.
Navigating the AI-Driven Writing Classroom from the University of Virginia’s Teaching Hub
A curated collection of resources for integrating generative AI into college writing instruction, including assignment guidelines, peer-review activities, and ethical frameworks.
Writing Assignments and Generative AI also from the University of Virginia’s Teaching Hub
Provides practical guidance for college instructors on using generative AI in writing courses, with sample policies, assignment ideas, and strategies for fostering ethical use.
