    • Suggestions from ChatGPT
      Expand to see list of assignment names & short descriptions
      • Assignment 1: Introduction to Generative AI
        Students explore foundational concepts, capabilities, and ethical implications of generative AI through readings, video lectures, class discussion, and a reflective essay.
      • Assignment 2: AI in Practice – Case Studies
        Students analyze discipline-specific case studies on real-world AI applications. They work in groups to present findings and submit an individual analytical report.
      • Assignment 3: AI Tool Exploration and Prompt Engineering
        Students experiment with AI tools (like ChatGPT, DALL·E) and learn prompt engineering through workshops and exercises. They submit prompt-output sets with analysis.
      • Assignment 4: Ethical AI Project
        Students design and develop an AI-based solution to a real-world problem, with an emphasis on ethical design. Work includes a proposal, group development, and a final presentation/report.
      • Assignment 5: Reflection and Future Directions
        Students keep a reflective journal, write a final essay about the future of AI in their field, and participate in a class discussion about their learning and ethical commitments.
