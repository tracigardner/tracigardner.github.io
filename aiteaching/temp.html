What prompts should a beginner use to begin interacting with an LLM?

These prompts are designed to give you a broad understanding of the LLM’s capabilities, its potential applications in education, and important considerations for its use. As you become more comfortable, you can start crafting more specific prompts related to your teaching needs.

Prompt Why It’s Helpful
”What are you capable of doing?" This prompt helps you understand the LLM’s capabilities and limitations.
"Can you explain how you work in simple terms?" This gives you a basic understanding of LLM functionality.
"What are some ways you could assist in [your subject area]?" Tailors the conversation to your specific field.
"Can you give me an example of how to write an effective prompt?" Helps you learn about prompt engineering from the AI itself.
"What are the ethical considerations I should keep in mind when using AI in education?" Encourages critical thinking about responsible AI use.
"Can you suggest a simple classroom activity that incorporates AI?" Provides practical ideas for implementation.
"What are some common misconceptions about AI that I should be aware of?" Helps clarify understanding and avoid pitfalls.
"How might AI change the way we assess student learning?" Explores potential impacts on a key aspect of education
"Can you summarize a complex topic in [your field] as if explaining it to a student?" Demonstrates the AI’s ability to explain concepts at different levels.
"What questions should I be asking that I haven’t thought of yet?" Encourages exploration of unknown areas and critical thinking.
