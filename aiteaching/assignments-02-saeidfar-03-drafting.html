Reading & Drafting Literacy Narratives

3.a. Reading Assignments

Objectives

  • Enhancing students’ reading comprehension using AI.
  • Making reading experience interactive and enjoyable using AI.

Activities

  • Part 1: Students read the assigned text carefully.
  • Part 2: Students work in small groups to answer the comprehension questions that they ask AI to generate using the following prompt or similar ones:

    Prompt Example: “Generate a series of comprehension questions based on the attached text... (prompt continues).”

  • Part 3: Each group presents their takeaways from the text with the rest of the class.

3.b. In-class half draft

Objectives

  • Practice drafting a text using an outline

Activities

  • Students use their bullet point outline and literacy map to draft their literacy narrative in class. They are not allowed to use AI for the half draft.
