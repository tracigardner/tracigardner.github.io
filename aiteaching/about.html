About This Site

The Teaching in the Age of Generative AI faculty cohort formed in May 2024. In partnership with the Provost’s Office, TLOS organized members with funding from an AI-centered course enhancement grant. This grant, supported by funds from the 4-VA initiative, will provide awardees with a summer stipend to design or redesign assignments, assessments, and/or classroom activities to leverage generative AI—and to ensure that AI is used responsibly and appropriately.

Grant recipients joined a cohort of faculty (listed in the Cohort Working Groups below) from a range of disciplines to deepen their understanding of generative AI, collaborate with colleagues to review and refine their course materials, and collectively develop a set of recommendations for other faculty at Virginia Tech to consider when integrating generative AI into their courses.
