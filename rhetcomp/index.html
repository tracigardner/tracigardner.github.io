Rhetcomp Googlegroup

I've set up a Googlegroup to help colleagues remain connected if/when Twitter goes away. The Rhetcomp list discusses teaching rhetoric and composition in higher ed—first-year composition (FYC), professional and technical writing (PTW), creative writing, and any other kind of writing you can come up with. This list promotes diversity, equity, inclusion, and accessibility.

How to Join

Visit https://groups.google.com/g/rhetcomp and use the Join the Group link.

Group Rules

These rules are evolving and will be updated as necessary.

  1. The list is carefully monitored and, if necessary, will switch to moderation to ensure a welcome space for collegial discussion. My decisions on moderation and removal are final.
  2. Begin your message with a hashtag that will help recovering Tweeters determine if the message is relevant (e.g., #TeamRhetoric #WomeninTC #CWCon ) 
  3. Keep messages reasonably short. This is a Twitter alternative, not a monograph alternative.
  4. Post only welcoming and diverse messages. I will remove racist, sexist, transphobic, ableist, and any other inappropriate messages. Hate posts are not tolerated.
  5. Don't be a jerk.

Sustainability & Longevity

As long as we have no coup d’etats or explosions of unmanageable hate, this list can remain open for colleagues to use.
