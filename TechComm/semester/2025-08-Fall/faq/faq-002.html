Due Dates & the Grace Period

Is this assignment due September 5th? Or do we have the whole semester?

The Subject Selection Message has a Target Due Date of September 5. The semester-long Ut Prosim projects are due on various dates throughout the term. A list of Target Due Dates for assignments through September 26 is on the Syllabus page. Scroll down past the course policies manual.

Is there an easily accessible list of deadlines for that assignment?

The deadlines for each week are available in the Module for that week. Additionally, a list of Target Due Dates for assignments through September 26 is on the Syllabus page. Scroll down past the course policies manual. More dates will be listed as I add the course material.

All of the assignments are due on the same day every week, so I’m struggling to find time.

Staggered deadlines—quizzes on Tuesday, discussions on Wednesday, projects on Thursday—rarely fit everyone’s schedules. Instead, each week has one Target Due Date at the end of the week. The idea is to give you the freedom to manage your own time each week. Plan your own milestones, and complete all work by that Target Due Date. If you need more time any week, use the Grace Period.

I'm still a little confused about the grace period deadline.

Basically, the grace period is a automatic extension on all course assignments (except the final exam). You don’t have to ask in advance or let me know you’ve decided to use it.

The Target Due Date for each week’s work is Friday. The grace period gives you THREE extra class days to turn your work in. It ends on the Wednesday following the Target Due Date.
