Feedback on Your Writing

Canvas Feedback

  • Canvas automatically sets some work Complete (or 1) when you submit it.
  • Canvas automatically sets missing work Incomplete (or 0) after the suggested due date.
  • Don’t panic about these 0 marks. You can still turn in the work until the end of the grace period.
  • Find the date for the end of the grace period in the “Available until” date on the assignment.

 

Specific Comments

 

Informal Messages (Use a comma)

  • Hi, Sam, (Text or email to a friend)
  • Hello, everyone, (Group message to classmates)
  • Hey, Coach, (Casual note or email)
  • Dear Mom, (Personal letter)
  • Hi, team, (Informal workplace message)

Note: In these informal examples, notice the comma after Hi, Hello, and Hey—and the comma after the name/group, too.
