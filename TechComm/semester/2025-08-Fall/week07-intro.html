Topic Overview

In this module, you’ll learn about the ways document design can make the difference between a successful document and a document that fails.

In technical writing, good design ensures readers can find and understand what they need without frustration. Module Learning Outcomes

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Analyze the needs and expectations of a non-expert audience for user-focused communication.
  • Apply document design principles (organization, layout, visuals) to make information clear and easy to navigate.
  • Revise content for plain language and usability to ensure accessibility and inclusivity.
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of your document in supporting action and understanding for a broad audience.
