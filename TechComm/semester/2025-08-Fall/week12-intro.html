Module Objectives for this Unit

By the end of this unit, you will be able to:

  • Research and explain a technical or complex subject clearly for a non-expert audience.
  • Describe how a topic addresses or relates to fairness, accessibility, and the well-being of communities.
  • Apply core technical writing skills—such as clarity, organization, and audience awareness—in support of socially meaningful communication.
