Why It Matters

Anyone who doesn’t earn a Complete on their first try at the informational report could end up and a zero for 20% of their course grade. I don't think that’s a fair representation of your work on the project.

Everything is working fine for the Homework, Quizzes, Check-Ins, and Self-Checks.

I’m quite worried about the fairness of the grades for the remaining Major Assignments.

What Changes

Changing the system to allow for partial credit.

1-Point to 100-Point Scale

Originally, I tried using the 0/1 system, simply adding partial scores across the levels in the rubric, shown in the second column of Table 1 below:

Table 1. Rubric Scale Comparison by Level
Level 1-Point Scale 100-Point Scale
Exceeds Expectations 0.12 12
Meets Expectations 0.10 10
Almost There 0.07   7
Needs Work 0.05   5
Missing 0.00   0

It worked, but it seemed silly to mess with all those decimal points. I switched to 100 points and adjusted the levels in the rubric, as shown in the third column of Table 1.

Nothing else needs to change. Everything still works mathematically.
