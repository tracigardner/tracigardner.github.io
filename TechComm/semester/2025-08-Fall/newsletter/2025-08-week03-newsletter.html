üîëKeys to Your Research Proposal

Today we’re starting a three-week project devoted to your Research Proposal for the Informational Report. Here are the key things to remember:

It’s related to the Ut Prosim project.

It maps out your research plan for the Informational Report for Non-Expert Readers.

It explains why your topic matters.

It’s presented in memo format.

It lays out your project’s scope, research questions, audience analysis, timeline, and initial sources.

It shows how your background prepares you to research this topic.

It describes how your work will support community needs.
