Topic Overview

In this module, you’ll learn how to present your ideas using plain language.

Plain language means writing that is clear, direct, and easy to understand. It avoids jargon and long, complicated sentences.

In technical writing, plain language helps readers—especially non-experts—quickly grasp important information and make decisions based on what they’ve read.

Module Learning Outcomes

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Write clearly and concisely using plain language techniques.
  • Revise texts to increase their readability.
