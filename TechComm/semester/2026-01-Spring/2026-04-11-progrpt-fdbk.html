I have added feedback on all of the Progress Reports. In this post, I’ll share details on your grades. Refer to the Feedback Notes on the Letters of Transmittal and the Progress Reports Announcement for basic details on the grading strategy I used.

Feedback on Your Progress Report

Find comments on your Progress Report in three possible places:

  • Annotations on your submission. Look for comments on or to the left of your document.

  • Comments in the rubric. Read the description listed in the rubric as well as any custom comments added for specific characteristics.

  • Summary comments that show when you look at the assignment.

Revision for the Progress Report

If you earned an 89 or less on your Progress Report, you can revise and resubmit your document. The grade for your revision will replace your current grade. There is no penalty for revision. If you decide not to revise, your grade will stand as is.

Progress Report Revision Dates

  • The deadline for revision of your Progress Report is Monday, April 20 at 11:59 PM.

  • The grace period for Progress Report revisions ends Thursday, April 23 at 11:59 PM.

How to Resubmit

If you eligible to submit a revision, go to the Progress Report on Informational Report, and upload your new attempt.
