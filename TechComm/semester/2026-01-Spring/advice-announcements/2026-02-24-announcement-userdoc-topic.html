Choosing Your User Document Topic

The Assignment

Write step-by-step instructions that guide a non-expert user through a task and a cover memo to Traci that explains your instructions.

Requirements

  • Connect directly to your Informational Report topic.
  • Address a different non-expert audience than your Informational Report.

Make It Concrete

Concrete choices mean naming

  • real people.
  • real roles.
  • real actions.

The more precise your answers, the stronger and clearer your writing becomes.

‚úÖ Quick Check

Ask yourself:

  • Is this task directly related to my report topic?
  • Is this written for a specific local group with a mailing address?
  • Is this audience different from my report audience?
  • Does this document help them complete a real task?

If the audience hasn’t changed, revise.

If the purpose is still explaining instead of guiding action, revise.

Your topic stays the same.

Your audience shifts from deciding to doing.
