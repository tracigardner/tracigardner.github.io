Why the sequence matters

Build expertise, test explanations, and improve clarity as you go.

Step 01

Subject Selection Form

Submit your topic for approval and explain your plan in a short form.

Step 02

Research Proposal

Define scope, questions, and sources—including a brief literature review.

Step 03

User Document

Create a quick, usable guide (instructions, checklist, or how-to handout).

Step 04

Progress Report

Report what’s done, what’s next, and what’s challenging.

Step 05

Informational Report

Explain your topic for non-expert decision-makers so they can act with confidence.

Step 06

Fact Sheet

Translate the essentials into a highly visual, easy-to-scan one-pager.
