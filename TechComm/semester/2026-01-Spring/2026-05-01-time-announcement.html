End-of-the-Term Feedback on Your Work

As we come to the end of the course, I need you to be aware of some details about feedback on the major projects you submit. Some of you are likely eager to know what your course grade will be, especially if you are trying to make a decision about withdrawing from the course or whether you want to revise the Fact Sheet for the final exam.

Here’s the basic situation: The closer to the last day of classes (May 6) you submit your project, the longer it will take for me to give you feedback.

I am returning work as fast as I can, but as more work comes in, I need more time to read and respond. Please understand that if you do not turn in your work until May 6, I won’t be able to get your work returned and your course grade estimated in time for you to decide on withdrawing.

The good news is that if you turn in your work by the Target Due Date (by 11:59 PM tonight, May 1) I guarantee that I will return it before May 6. I may not sleep 🛌💤this weekend, but I’ll get your Fact Sheet back to you!
