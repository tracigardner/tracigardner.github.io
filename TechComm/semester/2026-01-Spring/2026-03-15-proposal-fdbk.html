I have added feedback on all of the Research Proposals. In this post, I’ll share details on your grades and how to use the feedback to ensure you earn a great grade on your Informational Report for Non-Expert Readers.

Feedback on Your Proposal

Find comments on your proposals in three possible places:

  • Annotations on your submission. Look for comments on or to the left of your document.

  • Comments in the rubric. Read the description listed in the rubric as well as any custom comments added for specific characteristics.

  • Summary comments that show when you look at the assignment.

Revision for the Research Proposal

If you earned an 89 or less on your proposal, you can revise and resubmit your proposal. The grade for your revision will replace your current grade. There is no penalty for revision. If you decide not to revise, your grade will stand as is.

Proposal Revision Dates

  • The deadline for revision of your proposal is Tuesday, March 24, at 11:59 PM.

  • The grace period for proposal revisions is Friday, March 27, at 11:59 PM.
