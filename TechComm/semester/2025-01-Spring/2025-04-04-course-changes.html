In response to your feedback, I am making some changes to the course that I hope will help with the two main challenges you mentioned in last week’s Check-In Survey: (1) Group Challenges, and (2) Assignment Confusion.

Some Course Details

  • The Project Management unit requires group work. Collaborative project management is a program requirement. You cannot complete these projects individually.
  • The longer, detailed assignments will continue to be available because some students prefer the full details on the course work.

Assignment Changes

  1. The Recommendation Report Assignment is being replaced by the Fact Sheet Collection Assignment. This new assignment should relieve group challenges by shifting to more individual responsibility.

    Here are the important details:
    1. The Fact Sheet Collection is still a group assignment; however, I will grade it individually.
    2. Required Group Work: Each group must meet to make some basic decisions to coordinate the collection, including writing the front matter.
      1. Group members who do not contribute to group decision-making and group meetings earn an Incomplete on weekly activities.
      2. Group leaders may make binding decisions with the group members who do participate (with one exception, see iv).
      3. Binding decisions means that group members who choose not to participate are bound by the decisions that those who do contribute make.
      4. The Exception: Group members are free to choose their own fact sheet topics, as long as they do not choose a topic that has already been claimed (first come, first served).
    3. Required Individual Work: Each group member will write a fact sheet that is added to the collection.
      1. ⚠️ Group members who do not submit a draft of a fact sheet by the Target Due Date will earn an Incomplete. This requirement is intended to keep any group member from preventing the group from waiting to submit their work.
  2. Other assignments in the course will necessarily change. I will update assignments and other resources as quickly as I can. These changes mean the following for you:
    1. You will still use the research your groups gather this week.
    2. ⚠️ Do NOT work ahead. Give me a chance to update the relevant assignments.
    3. If you have not turned in your Group Proposal, continue that work. Here’s why:
      1. The website your group chooses will be the website your Fact Sheet Collection focuses on.
      2. If you choose not to participate in the proposal, you will still be required to use the website your group chooses.
