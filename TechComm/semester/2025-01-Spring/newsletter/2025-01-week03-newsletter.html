üì© What Annoys You Most in Communication

I asked what bothers you the most in workplace communication. The overwhelming response? 71% of you said unclear writing!

Other common frustrations:

  • Misspelled names or incorrect details (11%)
  • No subject line (9%)
  • Too short or lacking effort (6%)
  • Unreadable font size (3%)

üöÄ Tips for Writing That Stands Out:

  • Use clear structure ‚Äì Headings, bullet points, and logical flow matter.
  • Be direct but detailed ‚Äì Don’t assume the reader knows your context.
  • Prioritize readability ‚Äì Use formatting (headers, bullet points, bolding) to improve scanability.

In technical writing, clarity is everything‚Äîwhether in a report, email, or proposal. Keep these principles in mind to make your writing more effective!

üìå Understanding Letters, Memos, and Emails

Many of you (37%) said you need more clarity on workplace correspondence. Here’s a quick guide:

  • Letters: Formal, used for external communication.
  • Memos: Internal updates, usually brief.
  • Emails: Flexible, ranging from informal to highly professional.
