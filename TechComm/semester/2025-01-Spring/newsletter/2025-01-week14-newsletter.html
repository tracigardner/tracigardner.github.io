Course Grade Estimates Released

I have calculated your course progress as of noon on April 28, 2025. You can find your estimate in the rubric for the Course Grade Estimate, as of April 28 assignment on your Grades page in Canvas, calculated according to the Effort Expectations for Each Grade Level.
