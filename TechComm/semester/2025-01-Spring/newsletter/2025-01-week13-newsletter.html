üìà Improving Your Course Grade

To improve your grade, complete all assignments that remain open before the Grace Period closes at 11:59 PM on Friday, May 2.

First Priority:
Major Projects have the biggest impact on your course grade, so prioritize these tasks:
  • Survey of Website Writing In Your Field Informal Report (an individual project)
  • User Documentation Revision & Reflection Memo (an individual project)
  • Proposal for Your Recommendation Report (a group project)
  • Progress Report (a group project)
  • One of these options:
    • Recommendation Report (a group project)
    • Fact Sheet Collection (a group project)
Second Priority:
After the Major Projects, focus on any open Weekly Activities.

There’s one important consideration however. The Weekly Activities sometimes will help you with the Major Projects. For instance, Completing a Self-Check for each Major Project will help you determine if you have met all of its requirements.

üìÇ Individual vs. Group Projects

For the individual projects, you can work on these assignments on your own, at your own pace.

For the group projects, you need to coordinate with your group. The group activities cannot be completed individually.

  • If you are actively working with your group, continue collaborating and ensure your projects are submitted before the grace period ends (at 11:59 PM on May 2).
  • If you have not been working with your group, contact them immediately and ask how you can help with the projects. At this late point in the term, it may be difficult to collaborate effectively.
  • If you did not participate in a project that has already earned a Complete, you missed the opportunity to get credit for that project.
