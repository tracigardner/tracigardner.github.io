We’re officially halfway through the semester! Here’s how you reported feeling about the course:

  • 35% feel "Okay."

Overall, most of you are in a good place, but if you’re feeling overwhelmed, don’t hesitate to reach out for support—whether from your group, campus resources, or me.

üìö Keeping Up With the Work

As we move into the second half of the term, staying on track is key. Here’s where the class stands on keeping up with assignments:

  • 33% are staying on track and rarely need the Grace Period.
  • 57% are a little behind and need to catch up on one or two things.
  • 11% are a week or more behind.

If you need to catch up, now is a great time to set aside a few focused work sessions. Prioritize the most urgent tasks first, and remember that late work is still accepted within the Grace Period!

ü§ù Group Collaboration: Making Connections

With group projects in full swing, connecting with your team is critical. Here’s how many members you were able to connect with this past week:

  • 30% connected with everyone in their group.
  • 17% connected with all but one group member.
  • 20% connected with most members.
  • 15% connected with only one or two members.
  • 17% haven’t connected with anyone yet.

If you haven’t been able to connect with your group yet, now’s the time to check in! Send a message in Canvas, set up a quick meeting, or touch base in your group’s discussion space. Consistent communication will make your projects much easier.
