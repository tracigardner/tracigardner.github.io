üö® Navigating Group Conflicts

Noticed something off in your group? Don’t ignore it—addressing issues early prevents bigger problems later.

  1. Identify the concern: Is a team member unresponsive? Are tasks unclear?
  2. Document the issue in a shared space like Canvas discussions or a group doc.
  3. Check with your team: Does this issue need immediate attention, or can it be resolved over time?
  4. Brainstorm solutions: Should you adjust deadlines? Divide work differently?
  5. Decide on the best approach with a quick poll or discussion, and make sure everyone is on board with the plan.
  6. Keep track of decisions and check in later to see if the solution is working.
