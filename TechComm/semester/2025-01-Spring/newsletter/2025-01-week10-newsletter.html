Your responses highlight the ongoing difficulties of coordinating group work in an asynchronous course. Here’s what stood out:

  • Difficulty aligning schedules for meetings and collaboration
  • Uneven participation, with some members missing deadlines or not contributing
  • Frustration with having to wait on others to complete shared tasks
  • Requests for alternatives to group work when teams are non-functional
How I’m Responding
