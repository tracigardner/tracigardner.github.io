🧭 Avoiding the “5 Mistakes”: Let’s Get Specific

Survey responses show that only 36% of the class feels they’re doing very well or better at avoiding the common pitfalls from Week 1. Let’s break them down with quick reminders and tips:

⏱ Time & Pacing Mistakes

  • 📆 Skipping check-ins means I can’t support you early — these help me identify patterns and offer feedback before you fall too far behind.
  • ⚠️ Missing the end of the grace period = a potential 0. Some assignments (especially group-related) must be submitted before the grace window closes.
  • 📉 Assuming you’ll “catch up later” is risky. Consistent progress each week helps avoid last-minute stress and missed credits.
✅ Tip: Block out two or three short “writing work sessions” each week. Even 30–45 minutes can make a big difference.

📝 Submission & Revision Mistakes

  • ✔️ A Complete = meets all assignment requirements. Submitting something rushed or incomplete won’t earn credit.
  • 🛠️ No time for revision = trouble. Feedback helps you succeed — but only if you build in time to revise.
✅ Tip: After you submit an assignment, schedule a 15-minute window to review my feedback and plan one revision step.
