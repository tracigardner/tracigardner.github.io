Some of you are worried about your progress in the course. In particular, I’ve had some questions about whether an A is still possible if you miss one of the weekly assignments.

Let me put your worries at rest. Of course, it’s possible. One little mistake is not going to ruin your course grade. We all make mistakes.

Course Grade Information

Let me share some overall information about grades as well. Generally speaking, if you do the work in the course you have a 70% chance of earning a grade in the A range. If you don’t do the work, you likely have a course grade in the C or D range. People in the F range usually withdraw from the course.

How to Do Well in this Course

Remember that the major projects are the strongest influence on your grade. To earn an A or A-, you need to ensure you earn a Complete on all of the major projects. If you skip an entire project, then you will find an A grade out of reach. Missing a weekly activity, however, is not going to ruin anything.
