Is our primary research supposed to be real?

Someone asked:

Are we supposed to conduct the experiment/ observations we outlined in our try- it #17? I was able to outline a possible experiment for my topic, however I would most likely not be able to conduct it on campus. Is our recommendation hypothetical or are we supposed to collect raw data?

Traci responds:

Your project is supposed to be real, with real primary and secondary research sources. If you cannot conduct the research you originally proposed, you can choose another way to conduct your primary research.

Is there an example for the Recommendation Report?

Someone asked:

I am still really confused on the Recommendation Report. I am trying my best to fully understand, but I think if we were able to have an example on how it should be set up, that would be great.

Traci responds:

There is an example Recommendation Report in the textbook. It is labeled as Figure 18.8, and it begins on page 502 in the print edition.
