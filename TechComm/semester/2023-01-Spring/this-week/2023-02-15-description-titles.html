This title has a subject and purpose. It even mentioned a form of the word recommend. Those are all good things.

It leaves me with questions however. What kind of green materials? Where exactly are they being implemented and how? Sure, at Virginia Tech, but that‚Äôs a rather large location. More details and precision would help the reader understand what this document will be about.

This title also makes me wonder how this report relates to the assigned topic of food. There are no clues here to tell me.
