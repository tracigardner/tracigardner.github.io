What Makes a Successful Slideshow?

In the Week 6 Check-In Survey, I asked you to tell me what you thingk is most important to a successful slide presentation.

The majority (37%) of those who responded by Tuesday, February 28, identified using simple, uncluttered slide design as the most important characteristic.

Using simple, clearly relevant images (and avoiding decorative clipart) was the second most popular characteristic, chosen by 23% of those who responded.

The remaining four characteristics nearly tied, with using large fonts, strong contrast, avoiding typo, and including only one idea on each slide each slide all earning 10% each.

The truth is that all these characteristics matter, so aim to meet all of them in your Pitch.
