Advice on Your Working Recommendation Report Titles

Every working title submitted by Sunday, April 9, in the Week 11 Survey is listed below with some advice on whether it works as is or needs some additional details to be an effective title.

Strong Titles

These titles all make it clear the document focuses on recommendations. They clearly include a form of the word, which provides the purpose for the document. The rest of the title explains the subject.
