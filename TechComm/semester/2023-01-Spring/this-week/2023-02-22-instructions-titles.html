Individual Titles that Need Work

Each of the working titles below has a slightly different problem, so I‚Äôve added details for each one.

Need Details or Different Phrasing

Best Uses for a Kitchen Bowl
These three working titles suggest that the document is about uses of the kitchen bowl. A list of uses is not exactly the same as how to use the bowl. Try clarifying this title with a how-to or gerund structure.
Following The Rules
I‚Äôm not quite sure what the writer is going for here. This could be a title for anything. It is a gerund structure, but the reader has no way to know what rules the related document will discuss. It could be anything from a game of Monopoly to testing nuclear weapons. This title definitely needs more information.
Just Microwave it!
This title suggests a solution to a problem, which is solved by microwaving it. Unfortunately, the reader has no idea what ‚Äúit‚Äù is. It could be a CD or DVD, microwaved to see the mini-thunderstorm, or it could be a potato. There‚Äôs no way to tell‚Äîand it is not at all clear that the document provides instructions. This title needs to be revised so that the reader knows what they document is about. P.S. I am NOT encouraging anyone to microwave CDs or DVDs.
The Bottle Opener, But So Much More
This working title suggests that the document will provide a technical description of all the things this object is. It seems more like the title for a feature-by-feature description of the bottle opener. There‚Äôs no indication that the document will explain how to use the bottle opener. The title needs to be revised to indicate the document will provide instructions.

On the Wrong Topic

These titles are on the wrong topic. The assignment is to writing instructions that are related to food. Changing a car‚Äôs oil does not fulfill the requirements for the assignment.

This title is also on the wrong topic. It is clearly not related to food. Furthermore, it suggests that the document will be much longer and involved than required. This title also will not fulfill the requirements of the assignment.
