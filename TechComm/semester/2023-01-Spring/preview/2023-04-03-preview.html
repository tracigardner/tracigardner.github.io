Religious Events This Week

This may be the busiest week of the term for religious events. I’ve listed all of the holidays that I know about below.

  • Ramadan, a Muslim holiday, continues through Friday, April 21.
  • Passover, a Jewish holiday, begins at sundown on April 5th and lasts until sundown on the 13th.
  • Good Friday, a Christian holiday, is April 7.
  • Easter, a Christian holiday, is Sunday, April 9.
  • Palm Sunday in the Eastern Orthodox Church is Sunday, April 2.

Remember that you can use the grace period if you need so that you are able to mark the holidays you celebrate.
