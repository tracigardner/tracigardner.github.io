This announcement discusses what you can do if your course grade does not currently meet the goals that you set for yourself in the course. In addition to this information, be sure to read the Course Grade Estimates (as of Nov 17) Released Announcement.

Prioritizing Assignments

Complete all assignments that remain open before the Grace Period closes at 11:59 PM on Friday, December 1:

  • The Major Projects matter the most to your course grade, so prioritize these tasks:
    • Survey of Website Writing In Your Field Informal Report (an individual project)
    • User Documentation Revision & Reflection Memo (an individual project)
    • Proposal for Your Recommendation Report (a group project)
    • Progress Report (a group project)
    • Recommendation Report (a group project)
  • After the Major Projects, focus on the Weekly Activities. Note that the Weekly Activities sometimes will help you with the Major Projects.

Individual vs. Group Projects

For the individual projects, you can work on these assignments on your own, at your own pace.

For the group projects, you need to coordinate with your group. The group activities cannot be completed individually.

  • If you are actively working with your group, continue collaborating and ensure your projects are submitted before the grace period ends (at 11:59 PM on December 1).
  • If you have not been working with your group, contact them and ask how you can help with the projects. At this late point in the term, it may be difficult to collaborate effectively.
  • If you did not participate in a project that has already earned a Complete, you missed the opportunity to get credit for that project.

How to Find Open Assignments

To raise your grade from the estimate, complete the assignments that still have an open grace period. Prioritize the Major Projects, as they have the strongest influence on your course grade.

  • Go to the Assignments page and focus on the tasks that say “Available until.”
  • If the grace period has ended for an assignment, it will say “Closed” on the Assignments page. You can no longer do Assignments that have been ‘Closed.”
