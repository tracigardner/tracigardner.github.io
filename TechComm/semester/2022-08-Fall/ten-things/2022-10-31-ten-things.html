#1: Why Your Report Outline Matters

One glance at your outline tells me if you understand the report format. In fact, I can usually predict whether your report will earn a Complete just by whether your outline is fully detailed.

Here’s what you should include in your report outline:

  • A clear, specific title for your report.
  • All of the required sections of your report.
  • Specific subheadings that organize your sections.

Find the list of required sections on the Recommendation Report Criteria page.
