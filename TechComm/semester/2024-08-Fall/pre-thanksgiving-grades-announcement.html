How Was the Estimate Determined?

  • The rubric for the Current Course Grade Estimate is based on the Effort Expectations for Each Grade Level chart.
  • The rubric percentages come from the bottom of the Canvas Grades page (see an example under the Checking Your Progress heading).
  • I marked the percentage on your rubric according to the number listed. For instance, if you had 85.71% for Weekly Activities, I marked the B rating for that category in the rubric. I added the ratings according to the percentage cut-offs. I do not round or “bump up” the numbers.

I do have several strict guidelines, listed in the Short Guide under the Additional Grade Calculation Guidelines heading.

How Can I Raise My Grade?

To raise your grade from the estimate, complete the assignments that still have an open grace period. Prioritize the Major Projects, as they have the strongest influence on your course grade.

  • Go to the Assignments page and focus on the tasks that say “Available until.”
  • If the grace period has ended for an assignment, it will say “Closed” on the Assignments page. You can no longer work on or submit Assignments that have been ‘Closed.”

Remember that if your group has already earned a Complete on a project, you cannot contribute to that activity anymore.
