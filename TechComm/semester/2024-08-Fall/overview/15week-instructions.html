Holidays & Events This Week

  • Bodhi Day, which takes place on Sunday, December 8, is a Buddhist holiday marking the day that Gautama Buddha attained enlightenment.
  • The Feast of the Immaculate Conception is a Christian holiday that celebrates the conception of the Virgin Mary. It takes place on Sunday, December 8.
  • Human Rights Day is an international holiday observed on Sunday, December 10, the anniversary of the day in 1948 on which the United Nations adopted the Universal Declaration of Human Rights.
  • Gita Mahotsav, also known as Gita Jayanti, is a Hindu holy day that marks the day that the Bhagavad Gita dialogue took place. It is celebrated on Wednesday, December 11.
