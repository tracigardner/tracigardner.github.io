 Research Status

At this point, you should be finished or almost finished with your research for the Recommendation Report. So you can see how your progress compares to others in the course, I asked you to tell me about the status of your research. The results are in the graph below.

Description of Research Status Graph

Research Status

What is the status of the research for your group’s Recommendation Report?

Results:

  • 5%: We have not begun our research.
  • 13%: We need to gather more primary and secondary research before we can write our report.
  • 3%: We need to gather some secondary research before we can write our report.
  • 34%: We need to gather some primary research before we can write our report.
  • 34%: We have most of our primary and secondary research that we need to write our report.
  • 11%: We have all of the primary and secondary research that we need to write our report.
