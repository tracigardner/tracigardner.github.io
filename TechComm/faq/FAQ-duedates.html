How do due dates work?
There are three kinds of dates to keep in mind:
  • Suggested Due Dates: The date you should aim for. It’s suggested to keep you on track in the course.
  • Grace Period: The open period after the suggested due date, during which you can still turn in your work.
  • End of Grace Period/Deadline: The last moment you can turn in work for an assignment.
Read more about the system for due dates on page 6 of the Short Guide to the Course.
