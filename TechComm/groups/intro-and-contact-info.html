In your first post, be sure to mention these things:

  • Provide a bit of info about yourself (name, major, hometown, hobbies, anything that makes you YOU).
  • Talk about your schedule for the term and how you like to work so that you can begin to coordinate your workflows.

In your second (and additional) posts, connect with other group members and negotiate how you will work together next week, when we’ll have two group Discussions as your Try-Its for the week.

Optionally, you can talk about how to inform one another if you’ll be offline for several days as well as how to connect if someone isn’t responding. Make whatever communication plans you want as a team.
