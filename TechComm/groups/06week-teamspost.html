Task 1: Post Your Weekly Status Update

  1. Connect to Teams, and join your group’s channel.
  2. Find the “Week 6 Status Updates” post in your channel. I have added the header to keep all this week’s updates together in your channel.
  3. Click on the Reply button (marked by the blue arrow in the image below):
  4. As you did last week, share details on how you’re doing on work this week in your reply. Your post can include information on any (or all) of the following topics:
    • When you’re leaving for Spring Break and when you’ll be back.
    • What you’re currently working on for the course and how it’s going.
    • Successes in the course so far.
    • Challenges you’re trying to overcome in the course.
    • Anything group members might help you with.
    • Anything happening in your world that may impact your work this week (e.g., an exam in another class, going out of town, celebrating a holiday).
  5. Connect with other group members by doing some of the following:
    • Add a reply to any group member.
    • Add a Reaction (what Teams calls an emoji).
    • Offer to help someone.
    • Post any advice or suggestions.
    • Congratulate someone who achieves a goal.
  6. Aim to respond to every other group member by the end of the week.
