I have changed the way that Optional Discussions Posts will work in the course to make the conversations easier to manage. Instead of using Piazza, we will complete these conversations in Discussions in Canvas. The only thing changing is the tool that we will use. The general process of participating in these optional posts will remain the same.

Here are the revised guidelines, which are now updated in the Course Manual:

Every week, I will post optional discussion questions in Discusssions in Canvas. These questions will focus on topics such as the following:

  • issues that relate directly to projects you are working on.
  • ideas important to workplace writing and communication.
  • situations that explore workplace ethics and complex decisions.

You can add your thoughts to these posts by adding a reply. Just remember to use a strong subject line.

Feedback & Grading Process for Optional Discussions

This activity is completely optional, so there is no grading. It is simply an opportunity to explore ideas related to the class more fully and connect with others in the class. Do track your participation in your work log, as you will be reading and possibly writing in response.
