Many of you seem to need additional help with the Feedback Discussions, based on my spot check of the submissions and questions that I have been emailed. Please read through all of this announcement to find more about how Feedback Discussions work and what you need to do to earn a Complete.

What is the point of the Feedback Discussions?
These Discussions are essentially peer review. You share your draft with others in the class. You give two people feedback, and you get feedback from two people. It’s like asking a coworker to read over your draft and make suggestions.
