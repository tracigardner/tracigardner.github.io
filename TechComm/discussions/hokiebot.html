Tips on Using the HokieBot

I’m testing ways AI can assist in my classes. This course chatbot should only use course documents to answer your questions. Since AI can sometimes be inaccurate, verify details or ask it to name and quote the source.

If you ask HokieBot something outside the course materials, like questions about the Theory of Relativity or photosynthesis, it will prompt you to rephrase or ask a new question.

Do NOT enter any personal information, including your name, contact information, or details about your course progress or grade. Send any personal questions to Traci in Canvas’s Inbox.

If you are looking for a general AI chatbot, try Copilot. Be sure to login with your VT PID so that your writing is not cache or used for training purposes.
