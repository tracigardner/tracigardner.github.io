What You Said

I asked you to tell me what you would do or change to improve the Feedback Discussions. Here is a summary of the advice you gave me most often:

  • Modify when the work is due to encourage more timely feedback. There were various suggestions for how to change the timeline, such as earlier deadlines, no grace period, and more than one deadline.
  • Provide more structure for the feedback, such as suggesting people provide both praise for good work and critiques on what could be improved.
  • Encourage more specific questions about the drafts (rather than questions such as “How can I improve?”
  • Require more specific feedback. Eliminate overly simple responses like “I agree” and “This is good.”
  • Provide set guidelines for feedback so that there is a better idea of how to respond.
  • Be sure that the questions are open-ended so that people have to say more than “yes” or “no.”
