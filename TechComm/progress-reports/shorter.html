Progress Reports: Shorter Examples

Shorter reports tend to focus on a very specific aspect of a project, the work toward a specific milestone, or a set period of time (e.g., one week or a month). These shorter documents are frequently organized in simple sections, such as the following:

  • what work was accomplished
  • what work is still to be done
  • questions and/or concerns
This student-written example shows how one group of students reported on the work they had accomplished on their analysis project.
