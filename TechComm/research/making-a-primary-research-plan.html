What You Will Learn and Practice

  • How to analyze your audience and purpose so that you can choose supporting material that is convincing to your readers. [CLO 1]

What I Want You to Do

Identify the primary research strategies that will help you gather the information you need to establish the situation you are examining and to support the recommendation you are proposing.

Why I Want You to Do It

Primary research is a critical piece of your recommendation report. You need to research the situation completely to determine what is going on and how you can improve it. You need to decide on the best way to gather information about the situation and use that information to write your Instructions, which you will add to your Recommendation Report as an Appendix.

Where You Can Find Help

  • From Markel & Selber, Chapter 6 in the “Conducting Primary Research” section (pp. 135–147).
