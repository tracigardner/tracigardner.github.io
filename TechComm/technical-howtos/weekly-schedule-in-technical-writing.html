A general outline of what happens each week in the course. Check the Module Overview for each week for the specific due dates and information on the end of the grace period. Note that during the Summer Sessions, more than one Discussion post may be available.

Infographic Transcript

Day of the Week What Happens
Monday
  • Readings and activities posted in the Module for with week. Check the Module Overview for instructions.
  • Begin work on the readings and activities for the week.
  • Final, Finished Draft of major projects due by 11:59 PM, as noted on the course schedule . Grace period for these Final Drafts ends on Monday at 11:59 PM.
Tuesday
  • #TuesdayTutorial posted in Canvas Discussions. Reply if you want.
  • Continue work on the readings and activities for the week.
Wednesday
  • #WednesdayWrite posted in Canvas Discussions. Reply if you want.
  • Continue work on the readings and activities for the week.
Thursday
  • #ThursdayThought posted in Canvas Discussions. Reply if you want.
  • Continue work on the readings and activities for the week.
Friday
  • #FridayFact posted in Canvas Discussions. Reply if you want.
  • Continue work on the readings and activities for the week.
  • All Activities and the Self-Assessments for the week are due by 11:59 PM. Grace period for these items ends on Monday at 11:59 PM.
Weekend
  • #WeekendWatch posted in Canvas Discussions. Reply if you want.
  • Continue work on the readings and activities for the week.
