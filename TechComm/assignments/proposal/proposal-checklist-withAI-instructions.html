Why I Want You to Do It

In the workplace, you should be able to compare your work against a list of specifications to determine if your project is complete. This self-check works just that way: You should be able to confirm every one of the project criteria in the checklist before you consider the project ready to submit.

How You Do It

Preparing for the AI Self-Check

Why Login with VT.EDU? Virginia Tech has an agreement with Microsoft that protects your personal information and intellectual property rights.
  1. Review the Proposal Criteria, which outlines the requirements for the assignment.
  2. If necessary, save the Group Proposal as a PDF so that you can upload it to Copilot or the AI tool of your choice.
  3. Open Copilot in your web browser and log into the tool using your VT.EDU username and password.

Checking your Group Proposal

  1. Open the Copilot Prompt to Check Your Group Proposal PDF.
  2. Copy the prompt from the Copilot Prompt to Check Your Group Proposal PDF and paste it into the question area on Copilot.
  3. Click the Upload button in the Copilot question area, and upload the PDF draft of your Group Proposal.
  4. Click the Send button in Copilot and wait for the response. Here’s an example response to another student’s project.
  5. Click the Export button and save your response as a PDF.
  6. Upload the PDF you just exported to the first quiz question below so that I can see the responses Copilot gave you.

Answering the Self-Check Questions

  1. Answer the remaining quiz questions to indicate whether Copilot confirmed that you met the criteria for this project.
  2. Submit your checklist responses and check your point total for the activity:
    • If the checklist is marked 1 point for your Self-Check responses, your group has completed the activity successfully and are ready to submit your final version to the Proposal Assignment.
    • If the checklist is marked less than 1 full point, work with your group to revise the proposal to meet the criteria that you did you marked “False.” You should be able to confirm all the criteria before you submit your project.

How to Assess and Track Your Work

Work is always marked as either 1 for Complete or 0 for Incomplete:

  • Canvas will mark this activity Complete in Grades automatically when you confirm you have met all of the Proposal Criteria.
  • Canvas will mark this activity Incomplete in Grades automatically if you do not confirm that you have met all of the Proposal Criteria by the end of the grace period.
  • I will mark this activity Incomplete if I find your project does not meet the Proposal Criteria. You can submit an updated Self-Check after you revise your project.
