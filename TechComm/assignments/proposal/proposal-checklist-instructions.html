Relevant Course Objectives

  • Identify and define a problem or subject related to your particular field (or another specific problem according to your assignment’s directions) and assemble and analyze research related to this problem. [CLO 1]
  • Analyze the context of this problem or subject and determine appropriate audiences for the unit’s deliverables. [CLO 2]

  • Design strategies for evaluating the overall effectiveness of the suggested approaches to the researched problem. [CLO 5]

How You Do It

  1. Review the Proposal Criteria, which outlines the requirements for the assignment.
  2. Complete the checklist below, indicating the criteria for the project that you have met.
  3. Submit your checklist responses and check your point total for the activity:
    • If the checklist is marked 1 point for your Self-Check responses, your group has completed the activity successfully and are ready to submit your final version to the User Documentation Revision & Memo Assignment.
    • If the checklist is marked less than 1 full point, work with your group to revise ther proposal to meet the criteria that you did you marked “False”. You should be able to confirm all the criteria before you submit your project.

How to Assess and Track Your Work

Work is always marked as either 1 for Complete or 0 for Incomplete:

  • Canvas will mark this activity Complete in Grades automatically when you confirm you have met all of the Proposal Criteria.
  • Canvas will mark this activity Incomplete in Grades automatically if you do not confirm that you have met all of the Proposal Criteria by the end of the grace period.
  • I will mark this activity Incomplete if I find your project does not meet the Proposal Criteria. You can submit an updated Self-Check after you revise your project.
