The Pitch Presentation focuses on sharing basic information about the food-related challenge and the actions you recommend in response. Your audience for the presentation is the president of the university, since he has the ability to put your recommendations into action. Think of your pitch as something of a Shark Tank presentation. You have the Dr. Sands’ attention and just a few minutes to convince him to let you share more about your recommendation by submitting a report.

Pitch Criteria

You do not need to record your pitch with TikTok. Protect your privacy by NOT posting your classwork on your personal social media.

Length

Aim for 3 minutes or less (the maximum length of a TikTok video) for your presentation. You can run over a few seconds, but not a few minutes.
