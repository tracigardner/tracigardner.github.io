Length

The length of your description depends upon the concept that you explain and the examples that you include. Aim for at least one-page technical description, including the photos or images you choose.

Checklist Requirements

You are focusing on describing an object or process that relates to food in the form of a fact or info sheet. Your Full Draft Submission should meet the criteria below:

  • Has a title that indicates the nature and scope of the description clearly (p. 563).
  • Introduces the description by answering the following questions (all from p. 564), as appropriate:

    For an Object

    • What is the object?
    • What is the function of the object?
    • What does the object look like?
    • How does the object work?
    • What are the principal parts of the object?

    For a Process

    • What is the process?
    • What is the function of the process?
    • Where and when does the process take place?
    • Who or what performs the process?
    • How does the process work?
    • What are the principal steps in the process?
  • Provides detailed information (pp. 563–566).
  • Includes photos or images to illustrate the various aspects of the object or process.
  • Concludes by summarizing the major points of the object or process (p. 566).
