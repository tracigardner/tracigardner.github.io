Question 1

Write a sentence or two that you will use to introduce yourself.

Unless you are speaking to colleagues you work with every day, begin with an introduction: “Good morning. My name is Omar Castillo, and I’m the Director of Facilities here at United.” If you are using slides, include your name and position on the title slide.

Question 2

State the working title of your presentation, using the line breaks you will use on your slide.

Like all titles, titles of presentations should name the subject and purpose, such as “Replacing the HVAC System in Building 3: Findings from the Feasibility Study.” Include the title of your presentation on your title slide.

Question 3

What is the purpose of the presentation? Write an explanation in two or three sentences.

This explanation can be brief: ‘My purpose today is to present the results of the feasibility study carried out by the Facilities Group. As you may recall, last quarter we were charged with determining whether it would be wise to replace the HVAC system in Building 3.”
