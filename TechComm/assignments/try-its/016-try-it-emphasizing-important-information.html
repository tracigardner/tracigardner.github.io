Question 2

  1. What is the title of the presentation, as shown on the first slide? Copy and paste it in here.
  2. Next consider how the title emphasizes important information:
    • Does it clearly state the subject and purpose of the presentation?
    • Is it precise? Does it avoid using general terms?
    • Can it be paraphrased in a clear, meaningful sentence?
    • Is it too long? too short?
  3. Write two or three sentences that evaluate the title’s emphasis of important information.

For help, see pp. 198–199 in Markel & Selber.
