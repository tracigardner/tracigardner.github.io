How to Do It

Preparing for Your Week 08 Group Meeting

  1. Connect with the members of your group to arrange a meeting time that fits everyone’s schedules. You can meet using whatever system works best for your group. Here are some possibilities:
    • Gather in a Zoom or Teams meeting.
    • Post ideas in a Canvas Group Discussion. The following links can help you:
      • How do I view my Canvas groups as a student?
      • How do I create a discussion in a group?
    • Meet in person on campus or another convenient location.
  2. Before you meet, review the Recommendation Report Audience Analysis Form, choosing one of the options below:
    • Download Microsoft Word file
    • Make a Copy of the Google Doc version (File menu » Make a Copy)

During Your Week 08 Group Meeting

  1. Meet with your group as scheduled and arrange the work for this activity. You should be able to assign sections quickly. Alternately, you can collaborate during your meeting to fill out the form together.
  2. Choose a group member to keep minutes for your meeting, using the information on the Meeting Minutes Format page to record your group’s decisions.
  3. Complete the tasks listed in the Week 08 Meeting Agenda. As you work through the items, record your decisions in your Meeting Minutes.

After Your Week 08 Group Meeting

  1. Group Leader: Make an Announcement in your Group doing the following:
    • Title the Announcement: Week 8 Meeting Minutes.
    • Add any notes from the Minutes that you want to highlight as important.
    • Attach a copy of your Week 8 Meeting Minutes.
    Placing this information in an Announcement ensures that everyone has access to the minutes and the decisions that your group has made.
  2. Submit your completed Audience Analysis file as a PDF here once everyone in your group has completed their designated section(s).

Assessment

I will assess group members individually based on their participation. To earn a Complete, the section of the analysis you are responsible for must be filled out fully. I will mark your work for this Try-It Complete (or Incomplete) after the end of the Grace Period passes. Allow me several days to read and mark your work.
