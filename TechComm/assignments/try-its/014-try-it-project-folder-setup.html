  2. Give your team folder a title that combines the following, using hyphens between the items:

    • the semester
    • all your group members’ last names
    Here’s an example: 3764-F23-LastName1-LastName2-LastName3-LastName4
    This naming protocol assures that all of the folders will be gathered in one location.
  3. Share the folder with everyone in your group and with me (using the email address tengrrl@vt.edu).
  4. Upload or create your Meeting Minutes spreadsheet (from Try-It #13) in the folder you have created.
  5. Within that folder, create a Google Doc titled "Team Contact Info," add to it your names and contact information, and identify times you would be able to meet this week with your group.
