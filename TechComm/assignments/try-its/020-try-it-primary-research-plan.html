What You Need to Do

Begin collecting primary research for your Recommendation Report by deciding what each group member will do and setting a target due date for having your primary research gathered. Each group member must lead the work for one section of the Try-It.

How This Activity Connects to the Course

You and your group are working directly on your Recommendation Report by gathering the information that you will include in your report. Typically the primary research that you gather will help you demonstrate why the recommendations that you make are needed and/or why they will be effective.

Assessment

I will assess group members individually based on their participation. To earn a Complete, the section a group leader’s organizes must be filled out fully. I will mark your work for this Try-It Complete (or Incomplete) after the end of the Grace Period passes. Allow me several days to read and mark your work.
