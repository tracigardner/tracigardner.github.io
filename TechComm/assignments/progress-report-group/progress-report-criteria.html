The Progress Report project focuses on providing your reader with an honest assessment of your work on the Recommendation Report and your plans to complete the Recommendation Report.

Length

Write or compose as much as you need to, but be sure to include all of the required information. Generally progress reports for this assignment are 2 to 3 pages long. There is no specific length requirement.

  • Is written in standard memo format and document design, with appropriate spacing, headings, and page numbers, including the following:
    • Single space within section paragraphs.
    • Double space between sections and between paragraphs.
  • Includes the following sections:
    • An Introduction
      • INTRODUCTION First paragraph reminds the reader of the project and summarizes the work that has been accomplished so far. Second paragraph is the PURPOSE STATEMENT, which identifies the purpose of the document.
    • A Work Completed Section
      • First section is WORK COMPLETED. In this section, tell the reader what you've been able to accomplish so far. Use concrete details. If you have subsections, make sure you include a brief sentence of introduction between your main heading and subheadings. Notice that the main heading is larger than the text. The secondary headings are also larger, but not as large as the main heading.
    • A Work Scheduled Section that includes
      • The WORK SCHEDULED section identifies work you still have left to do. You can handle this section by using a GANTT CHART to visually identify your dates and goals. If you use such a chart, you must introduce it with a simple sentence between it and the main heading. Notice that this main heading matches the size/style of the WORK COMPLETED heading.
