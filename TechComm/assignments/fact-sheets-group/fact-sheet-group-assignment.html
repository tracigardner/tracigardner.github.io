What I Want You to Do

You and the members of your group will write a collection of fact sheets that focus on usability, document design, and accessibility principles. Your group will focus on the website identified in your Proposal. Each team member will create one fact sheet using that site as an example. The final collection will include a title page, a table of contents, and a letter of transmittal.

Each fact sheet should:

  • Focus on one principle (e.g., accessible color contrast, alt text, or heading use)
  • Be exactly two pages in length
  • Include clear examples from the chosen website
  • Use consistent formatting and design across the group’s collection

Your audience will be employees responsible for maintaining the chosen website. Your fact sheets should help them recognize and apply improvements to the site’s design and accessibility.

Why I Want You to Do It

You will write longer documents in the workplace, usually with a team of people.
