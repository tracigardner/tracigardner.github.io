This self-check quiz consists of thirteen true/false questions and one multiple choice question. You can complete the self-check as many times as you like, until the grace period ends.

Self-Check Information

  1. Compare your project to the checklist below, indicating the criteria for the project that you have met. You can use either of these methods:
    • Compare the criteria to your draft yourself.
    • Use AI to help you compare your draft to the criteria, using these prompts and instructions.
  2. Submit your responses and check your point total for the activity:
    • If the checklist is marked 1 point (Complete), you are ready to submit your draft to the Research Proposal assignment.
    • If the checklist is marked less than 1 point, revise your project to meet the criteria that you marked “False.” You should be able to confirm all the criteria before you submit your project.

Grading

Canvas automatically grades your Self-Check when you submit it. To earn a Complete, you need to score a 1/1. Any figure lower than 1 will be an Incomplete.
