Testing the Subject & Audience for Your Informational Report

I asked a lot of you to narrow the subject and audience for your Informational Report. You need to focus more BEFORE you submit your Research Proposal. If your subject and audience are not on target, you’ll need to revise your proposal.

Use this process to test your subject and audience. You must be able to answer “Yes” to every question to have a subject that works.

The Major Test

Does your subject specifically relate to your major?

Compare your answer:

  • If you answered, “Yes,” move to the Technical Topic Test.
  • If you answered, “No,” stop and choose a subject that does.

Example

I’m majoring in Technical & Scientific Communication.

The Technical Topic Test

Does your subject fit in one of these categories?


It’s an object in my field.

It’s a practice in my field.

It’s an idea in my field.

Compare your answer:
  • If you answered, “Yes,” move to the next step.
  • If you answered, “No,” stop and choose a subject that does.

Compare your answer:
