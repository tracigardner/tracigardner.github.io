1. Save your project as a PDF so that you can upload it to Copilot or the AI tool of your choice.
  2. Open the AI Prompt to Check Your Research Proposal.
  3. Open Copilot in your web browser and log into the tool using your VT.EDU username and password.
  4. Copy the prompt from the AI Prompt to Check Your Research Proposal and paste it into the question area on Copilot.
  5. Fill in the blank in the first line of the prompt with the name of your career field (e.g., electrical engineer, software developer, financial analyst)
  6. Click the Upload button in the Copilot question area, and upload the PDF draft of your Research Proposal.
  7. Click the Send button in Copilot and wait for the response.
  8. Click the Export button to save your response as a PDF. Save it in case I ask for it later.
  9. Go back to the Self-Check, and follow the rest of the instructions in the Self-Check to confirm the criteria for the project.
