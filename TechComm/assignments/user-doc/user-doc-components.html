  1. Table of Contents (Optional, for multi-task, multi-section, or manual-length documents).
  2. Introduction: State task and scope, audience/background needs, purpose/outcome, when to use, and an overview.
  3. General Notices: Notes, Cautions, Warnings, Dangers to highlight risks or key exceptions.
  4. Technical Background or Theory (Optional): Concepts/definitions needed to understand the procedure.
  5. List of Equipment and Supplies: Tools, equipment, and consumables (add specs like brand, size, amount as needed).
  6. Directions (Steps): Step-by-step instructions with appropriate formatting and visuals:
    • Fixed-order steps: Numbered list.
    • Variable-order steps: Bulleted list.
    • Alternate steps: Bulleted options with “OR.”
    • Nested steps: Indented substeps (a, b, c...).
    • “Stepless” instructions: General guidance when steps don’t fit.
  7. Conclusion: Wrap-up, troubleshooting guidance, and contact information.
  8. Back Matter (Optional): References, glossary/appendix, index, technical specifications.
