There are two options for all of the Self-Checks in the course:

  • You can do the self-check yourself by comparing the criteria to the information in your project.
  • You can use AI to compare the criteria to the information in your project.

You can choose either option. I am offering both so that you do not have to use AI if you don’t want to. The project criteria are the same for both. The self-check that you do NOT complete is dropped.

Some Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my mind?
Absolutely. If you start out with the AI Self-Check, you can switch to the regular self-check and vice-versa. You simply need to earn a 1 (Complete) on one of the options.
Does it matter which AI tool I use?
Maybe. I design our activities to be compatible with Copilot to streamline the activities I ask you to complete. You are welcome to use other AI tools that you have access to. If an alternative tool cannot accomplish an assigned task however, you’ll need to switch back to Copilot.
