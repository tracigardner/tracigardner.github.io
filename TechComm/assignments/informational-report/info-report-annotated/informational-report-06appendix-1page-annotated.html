Purpose: The Appendix (plural: Appendices) for your report includes supplementary materials that support the report but are too detailed or lengthy to include in the main body. This information can include raw data, charts, documents, or additional explanations that help readers better understand the report’s content.

Length: Varies depending upon the materials you provide. Use as much space as needed to present the information.

Section Content: See the assignment and textbook.

Titles:

  • If there is only one appendix (this page), use Appendix: and the title of the appendix.
  • If there is more than one appendix, use Appendix A: and the title of the appendix for the first appendix. Increment the letter for each additional appendix.
