What I Want You to Do

Your Recommendation You and the members of your group will write a recommendation report that analyzes the usability of a website that a Virginia Tech student would use in the course of their academic work at the university.

You will include customary sections (e.g., intro, methods, results, conclusions, and recommendations), along with front and back matter. Your report will probably be 10–15 pages long, including all sections.

Why I Want You to Do It

You will write longer documents in the workplace, usually with a team of people. This assignment examines the various parts that comprise a long report and asks you to practice formatting and arranging a long document.

How You Do It

Important Note: Because the Recommendation Report is long with many sections, instructions and advice for each of the sections are listed separately for this assignment. Visit each of the pages to be sure that you include the required information for each section.

  1. Return to your Recommendation Report Outline and begin adding information to the document until you have included all of the sections listed in the following substeps:
    1. Compose the body of your Recommendation Report:
      • Writing Your Introduction
      • Writing Your Methods Section
      • Writing Your Results Section
      • Writing Your Conclusions Section
      • Writing Your Recommendations Section
    2. Compose the Front Matter for your Recommendation Report:
      • Writing Your Letter of Transmittal
      • Writing Your Title Page
      • Writing Your Abstract
      • Creating Your Table of Contents
      • Writing Your Executive Summary
    3. Compose the Back Matter of your Recommendation Report:
      • Writing Your References
      • Writing Your Appendixes
  2. Success Tip
    You should be able to answer “True” to each question before you submit your work to the Full Draft Submission. If you do not meet each of the criteria listed, your draft will be marked Incomplete and you’ll need to revise.
    Review your draft by comparing it to the Recommendation Self-Check.
  3. Choose one of these options based on your Self-Check:
    • If you answered “True” to every question, move on to the next step. You’re ready to submit your work.
    • If you did not answer “True” to every question, follow this process:
      • Return to your draft and revise so that you can answer “True.”
      • Review your draft with the Recommendation Self-Check after you revise.
        • If you answered “True” to every question, move on to the next step. You’re ready to submit your work.
        • If you did not answer “True” to every question, continue revising until you can.
  4. Submit your Full Outline here once you are ready. See How do I submit an online assignment? if you need help with Canvas.
