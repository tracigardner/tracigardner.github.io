How You Do It

Compose an informal list of the secondary resources that you have found for your report. For each resource, tell me the following:

  • The author and title
  • The kind of source (for instance, a book, a website, a journal article)
  • How you think you will use the resource (for instance, as background, to support a recommendation)

This is a working list. You can add or remove resources as you continue work on your report. You do NOT need formal citations at this point, but you can submit them if you have them already.
