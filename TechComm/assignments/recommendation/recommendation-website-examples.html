Kinds of Websites That Work

Compare your recommendation report topic to the ideas listed below. The list focuses on general kinds of businesses, nonprofits, community groups, and other organizations. The list is not exhaustive. It simply includes ideas that I have brainstormed. Your job is to find a specific business, nonprofit, or other group that meets all the requirements for the project.

If you are looking for additional ideas, check the Downtown Blacksburg website, Downtown Christiansburg website, and the Business Directory for Montgomery County.

  • Brewpubs

  • Consignment stores

  • Community and public health services
