The Recommendation Report is the culmination of all the projects completed in the course. To meet the expectations of the project you will need to do the following:

  • Choose a very specific and personally observable topic, following the instructions on the “Recommendation Report Overview” page and using the Recommendation Topic Examples for reference.
  • Conduct secondary research to find supporting information written by other people, typically in books, articles, and websites.
  • Plan and conduct primary research, following detailed instructions, to demonstrate the problematic situation addressed in the report.
  • Incorporate technical description of the objects or processes involved in the problematic situation and recommendations.
  • Write a formal recommendation report that meets the criteria explained in the next section.

Recommendation Report Criteria

Length

With examples and appropriate formatting, your report will likely be close to 15 single-spaced pages long, though there is not a minimum or maximum page length. Write as much as you need to, but be sure to include all of the required information.
