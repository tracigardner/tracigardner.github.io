Overview

This optional peer review discussion gives you a place to post your working draft for feedback and, in response, to provide feedback to someone else in the course. Remember that if you post a draft, you are committing to providing someone else feedback.

How You Do It

  1. Add a reply below that tells your peer review readers about your draft and ask any questions that you have about it.
  2. Attach the current draft of your project to your reply for feedback.
  3. Upload your draft with the cover letter to this discussion for feedback.
  4. Look through the other replies after you post to find someone who has not received feedback yet.
  5. Read that personâ€™s reply and draft, and add a reply to their post with your feedback.
    • Posting a reply with your feedback is necessary since it indicates which students have gotten feedback and which have not. You may be able to comment on the document itself as well.
