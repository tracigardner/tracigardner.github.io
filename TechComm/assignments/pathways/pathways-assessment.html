What I Want You to Do

Think about your accomplishments in the course and assess your ability to complete the tasks outlined in the following questions. Your response will not damage your course points total, no matter how you answer.

Why I Want You to Do It

This is a Pathways course meeting the Discourse (Area 1) goals, and I am required to report on what students learn in the course. I need you to tell me about how well you meet the student learning outcomes for this course. Your response will help me improve the course to help students meet these learning outcomes as they complete the course.

How You Do It

  1. Reflect on your work in the entire course as you answer the questions below.

  2. Answer the questions honestly. Your responses will not influence your course grade.

Obtaining the Points for Your Work

  • Be sure to track your work in your Weekly Work Log.
  • Watch for feedback comments from me—find feedback in annotations and comments on your submissions in Canvas.

Work is always marked as either 1 for Complete or 0 for Incomplete:

  • I will mark this activity Complete in Canvas Grades, usually within 24–48 hours after the end of the grace period.
  • I will mark this activity Incomplete in Canvas Grades if you do not submit your draft by the end of the grace period.
