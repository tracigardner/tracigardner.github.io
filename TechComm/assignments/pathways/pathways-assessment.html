What I Want You to Do

Why I Want You to Do It

This is a Pathways course meeting the Discourse (Area 1) goals. I want you to tell me about how well you meet the student learning outcomes for this course. Your response will help me improve the course to help students meet these learning outcomes as they complete the course.

How You Do It

  1. Reflect on your work in the entire course as you answer the questions below.

  2. Answer the questions honestly. You will earn points for your Self-Assessment regardless of your response.
