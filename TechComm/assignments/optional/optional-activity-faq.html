I missed some of the optional readings for February? Is every activity required to earn an A?
No, you can do some basic math to figure out what is required. The weekly modules guarantee you a B, or 830 points, if you do all the work. An A requires 930 points or more, and there are 170 optional points in total. Math: 930 -830 = 100 optional points needed. Since there are 170 optional points available, there are 70 more points than you need for an A.
