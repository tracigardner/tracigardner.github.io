Drafting Usability Test Scripts with AI

What I Want Your to Do

In this assignment, you will collaborate with an AI writing tool (such as ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, or Gemini) to draft a usability test script for a real or simulated interface (e.g., a website, form, app, or product). Your goal is to generate a draft using AI and then refine it based on your understanding of effective usability testing.

Why I Want You to Do It

  • Practice writing clear, actionable usability tasks and questions
  • Evaluate the usefulness and limitations of AI-generated writing
  • Reflect on your role in directing and improving AI output

How to Do It

  1. Choose a Target Interface
    Select a digital interface you'd like to test (e.g., a university website, an app, etc.).
  2. Generate a Draft Script with AI
    Use an AI tool to create a usability test script. Include in your prompt:
    • Name or description of the interface
    • Target users (e.g., students, first-time visitors)
    • Goals of the test (e.g., navigation, finding info)
    Example Prompt:
    Write a usability test script for evaluating how easily college students can find academic advising information on [university website]. Include an intro, 3 tasks, and follow-up questions.
  3. Revise the Script
    Refine the AI-generated draft to improve clarity, realism, and tone. Your final script should include:
    • Short intro for test participants
    • 3–5 action-based usability tasks
    • Follow-up questions for feedback
  4. Document Your AI Collaboration (Instead of a Reflection)
    Log your AI interaction using the AI Collaboration Log format. Your log should include:
    • The AI tool(s) you used
    • Your prompt(s)
    • A summary or example of the AI-generated output
    • What you revised or rejected
    • Why you made those changes
    • Any ethical or technical considerations that came up

    Submit your AI Collaboration Log as an appendix to your assignment (in the same document or as a separate file).

    If you've used AI logs in previous assignments, label this one clearly (e.g., “Usability Test Script – AI Collaboration Log”).

Deliverables

  • Final revised usability test script
  • Copy or screenshot of the original AI-generated draft
  • AI Collaboration Log entry (attached as an appendix)
