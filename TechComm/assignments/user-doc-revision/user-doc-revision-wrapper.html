What You Need to Do

Reflect on the work that you put into your User Documentation Revision & Memo Assignment and then record some basic information about how you worked. You’ll return to this project wrapper after you receive feedback on your project as you complete the UX Project Feedback Wrapper.

How This Activity Connects to the Course

For each Major Project in the class, you’ll complete a wrapper when you turn in your project and then another when you get feedback on your submission. These wrappers ask you to reflect on how you plan, work, adjust, and evaluate your writing and project management in this course. They should help you recognize strategies that work for you and strategies that you need to change or improve.

Instructions

  1. Answer each of the questions below to the best of your ability.
  2. Know that your answers do not need to be exact. You’ll notice ranges of numbers for some questions.
  3. Include enough details for someone else to understand how much effort you put in as you worked.

Assessment

Canvas automatically marks your work Complete when you submit it.

It’s okay if you make mistakes. The point of this activity is to learn more about how you write, and we all realize we didn’t understand what we were doing when we look back on something later.

On the other hand, if you have not put in an effort to do the work, I may remove the Complete mark. Mistakes are fine. Not doing the work is not.
