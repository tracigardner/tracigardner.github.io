How You Do It

  1. Create a word processor file for your draft letter.

    2. How I Use Your Draft Letter

    Your draft letter tells me about your work on the Pitch. You will ultimately include parts of your script in your Recommendation Report, so this letter is your chance to ask for advice to improve it before turning in the full report.

  2. Write a draft letter to me (one page or less) that includes the following information:
    1. Tell me any general information that you want them to know about your presentation.
    2. Point out the part of the presentation that shows your best work, and explain why.
    3. Let me know one specific aspect or part of the presentation that you want to improve, and explain why.
    4. Add any final thought in a last paragraph, and close your letter.
  3. Check your cover letter and revise if it is longer than one page long.
  4. Save your draft letter.
  5. Turn in your draft letter and your presentation video.
