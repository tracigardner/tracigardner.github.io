This project focuses on researching and analyzing four different webpages to learn more about some of the types of writing produced by those who work in your field. After gathering information about the types of writing, you will write an informal report in memo format.

Length

Write or compose as much as you need to, but be sure to include all of the required information. There is no specific length requirement. Just be sure to include the required sections and full details on the four different types of writing.

  • Summarize each type of writing you learned about by including the following:
    • a heading that identifies the type of writing
    • a separate paragraph under the heading that identifies these 5 items in paragraph form:
      • genre (what type of writing is it?)
      • purpose of the type of writing (what is it meant to do?)
      • typical audience for it
      • usual mode of delivery (paper, blog, database, etc.)
      • usual length of the type of writing
  • Provides conclusions drawn from the research (what you learned) under the Conclusions heading.
  • Ends the memo with a conclusion.
