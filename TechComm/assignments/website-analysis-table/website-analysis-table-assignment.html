For this assignment, you are tasked with finding out about the types of writing produced by those who work in your field. You have two choices for finding this information:

  • You can interview someone working in your intended field.
    • This person can be a friend, a family member, or someone you know through past internships or co-ops.
    • This choice will allow you to ask specialized questions to find the information you need for the assignment.
    • You can interview in person, via Zoom, or via email.
    • If you choose this option, read Research: Interviews.
  • You can do all of your research online.
    • This choice might require you to do additional digging to ensure that you find the info needed for the assignment.

This assignment has two parts: (a) a cover memo and (b) a data table. Together, they form the two components of an informal report. You are to use the attached Excel template for your report.

Your report should include at least FOUR types of writing.

Parts of the Report

Cover Memo

  • The cover memo is located on the first tab of the attached Excel file. (Tabs are at the bottom of the sheet.)
  • Write your cover memo AFTER you have created your table.
  • Follow this structure:
    • Intro:
      • Explain the purpose of your project and summarize what you discovered.
      • This can be a single sentence or two sentences.
      • You can even start like this: The purpose of this report is to X. Researching for the report, I discovered Y.
    • Body:
      • Your body will consist of 6 sections.
      • Approach: Identify and discuss the approach you took to finding the information. Did you interview someone? If so, who? Where do they work? What's their job title? How do you know them? If you researched online, where did you find the information? Identify your sources in writing. You don't have to list all of the biblio material. Just indicate the source titles.
      • Types of Writing: In separate sections, each with a heading that identifies the type of writing, summarize the various types of writing you learned about.
      • Conclusions (use this as your heading; note the "S"): End the body section by discussing briefly what you learned from the project about the types of writing you might be producing in the workplace.
    • Conclusion: End cordially and provide your contact information.

Data Table

  • The data table is located on the second tab of the attached Excel template.
  • Although it follows the cover memo, it must be created first because the memo is based on information you will have learned to produce the table.
  • Your data table will cover these items for each of your writing types:
    • Writing Type (i.e., report, memo, etc.)
    • Purpose
    • Audience
    • Medium (How is it usually delivered?)
    • Length
