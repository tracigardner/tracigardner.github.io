Your Audience

Write your memo to Traci Gardner, your teacher for the course. I want to know how your research and preliminary work on the Informational Report is going.

Tell me about what has gone well, but also explain anything that has gone wrong and tell me what you are doing to address the challenge.

Progress Report Structure

Your progress report must include these required components, in this order:

  • Memo Headers: To, From, Date, Subject.
  • Introduction: Purpose statement and overview of your progress on the Informational Report for Non-Expert Readers. No heading.
  • Work Completed: What’s done so far—Use specific details!
  • Work Scheduled: What’s left to do, presented in an updated Gantt Chart. Include an introductory sentence.
  • Problems Encountered (optional): What challenges have you encountered and what strategies have your chosen to address them.
  • Conclusion: Draw ideas together and provide contact info. No heading.

Assignment Requirements

Basic Requirements

  • Genre: Memo [set up as a memo from you to your instructor; get the teacher’s name correct!].
  • Length: Usually 1.5–2 pages (including Gantt chart).
  • Plain Language: Use short sentences and other plain language techniques. Avoid jargon.
  • Document Design:
    • Format headings, spacing, and design consistently with appropriate contrast.
    • Chunk paragraphs to enhance readability.
    • Use lists, horizontal rules, and boxes as effective visual elements.
    • Employ CRAP (Contrast, Repetition, Alignment, & Proximity) Design Principles.

Formatting Requirements

  • Format: Text running from left to right margin, hierarchical headings, text wrapped around images, and 1" margins all around.
  • Spacing:
    • Single or 1.15 spacing between lines.
    • Double space between paragraphs and before and after headings and lists.
  • Pagination: Upper right or bottom right.
  • Paragraphs: Do not indent.
  • Font Choices: Arial, Calibri, Verdana, Aptos, or Helvetica.
  • Font Sizes:
    • MEMO Title: 20 pt bold.
    • To, From, Date, and Subject: 12pt bold.
    • Internal headings: 16 pt bold.
    • Body text: 12 pt.
    • Captions (if used): 10 pt italic.
  • Visuals: Include one Gantt chart only—no additional visuals required.

Success Tips

  • Clear and objective tone.
  • Specific details: names, dates, tasks.
  • Honest reflection on progress.
  • Headings and lists for easy reading.
  • Focused on facts—not filler.
