These Discussions focus on ethical dilemmas that AI can bring up for technical writers:

Your Code of Ethics
What policies and standards does your intended profession suggest for writing, including the use of generative AI? Students identify their own codes, compare them to policies for other members of their group, and create an interdisciplinary code of ethics that will guide their work.
The Ethical Dilemma of AI and the Absent Team Member
A team member has unexpectedly gone missing, and no one else at the company has the specific subject area knowledge needed to write a user manual for a submersible. Can AI solve the problem?
Balancing AI Efficiency with Writer Autonomy
Should a company shift to AI for all documentation to save money and increase efficiency? Opponents share concerns about the accuracy and clarity, the loss of the team’s unique writing style, and job security for human members of the team.
AI and Quality Standards in Documentation
An advanced AI system helps create and manage technical specifications and programming instructions. Now the system recommends that it rewrite sections of a manual to improve clarity and streamline information based on user feedback data. Should it be trusted?
Bias in AI-Generated Text
After a few months of using generative AI, writers notice that some documents contain subtle biases, stereotypes, and occasionally discriminatory language. How should they proceed?
AI and Data Privacy in Customer Chat
A new AI response chatbot has customers demanding more transparency and assurance that their privacy is being protected. What can the company do to convince customers their personal information is safe?
AI and Emotional Communication with Clients
An AI bot has recently mishandled several emotionally-charged messages from clients concerned about delays, cost overruns, and safety. What can the firm do to ensure clients are happy and that AI handles emotional messages more empathetically?
AI Disclosure in Agency Communication
Before implementing Copilot, a communications team considers the implications of using AI to generate and revise messages. Should they disclose the use of AI to donors and stakeholders?
The Environmental Impact of AI
Environmental advocacy groups and concerned customers are questioning the carbon footprint and energy use associated with the AI models used by a local company. What should the company do to address the concerns?
