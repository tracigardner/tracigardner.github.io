Directions and instructions are everywhere, from gas pumps to microwave meals. IKEA instructions are famous for their line drawings with no words. Directions for technology can be impossibly difficult to follow.

Today’s post considers one of the decisions you will make as you work on the User Manual. As you work on the steps of your instructions, you have to decide whether you will use a sequence or separate tasks.

Watch this Lynda.com video on List Steps (free with your VT.edu login) and consider the explanation for how to make your decision—essentially they video shares instructions for how to write your instructions! After you view the video, you can comment on the strategies that are used to organize instructions in the video, OR tell us about instructions and directions you have interacted with what used sequences or separate tasks.
