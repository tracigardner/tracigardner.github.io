Next week you will begin working on your Recommendation Report. As you review sources for your report, watch for stories or situations that will allow you to tell a story. Stories are a great way to grab your readers’ attention and interest them in your topic.

The Lynda.com video Storytelling at Work (2m39s) outlines why stories work and suggests some ways that you can tell stories that will help you meet your goal.

After watching the video, you can comment by telling a story about yourself as a writer, telling a story about a situation you observed or heard about related to writing in the workplace, or telling a story related to the topic you’ve chosen for your Recommendation Report.
