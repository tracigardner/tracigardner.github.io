The YouTube video Scientific Poster Design—Good and Bad Examples reviews the do’s and don’ts of research poster design. The biologist in the video demonstrates the tips with posters he has made and presented from his undergraduate days to the present. He also discusses how infographics relate to research posters.

The video is part of a series on how to use InDesign to create academic posters, but this particular video focuses on design choices (and not the software). Watch the related videos in the series if you are interested.
