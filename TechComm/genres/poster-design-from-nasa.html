The Washington NASA Space Grant Consortium’s site on The Basics of Poster Design is intended for student interns who will create research posters.

The site includes the following sections:

  • Getting started
  • Organizing your research
  • Choosing your tools
  • Mastering the basics
  • Creating design unity
  • Some final tips
  • More design resources & software tutorials

Review the information on the site for additional tips and advice. Compare what you find to the resources shared last week and determine the best strategies for your research poster.
