As we read the textbook, I will ask you to review the information and then answer some questions related to the reading. The kinds of questions will vary from one chapter to the next. The goal behind all of the chapter review activities is to help you think about, understand, and/or apply the ideas from the readings.

Your writing can be informal. Just be sure that I can understand what you have to say.

How to Assess & Track Your Work

You track and grade your own work in this course. Be sure to complete the following tasks:

  • Track your work in your Weekly Work Log.
  • Watch for occasional feedback on your work. I do not comment on every review, but I do read them all.

Work is always marked as either 1 for Complete or 0 for Incomplete:

  • Canvas will automatically mark this activity Complete after you submit your work.
  • Canvas will automatically mark this activity Incomplete if you do not submit your work by the end of the grace period.
