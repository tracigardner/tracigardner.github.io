How You Do It

  1. Go on a hunt on campus, wherever you live, or online for a good or bad visual. It can be any kind of visual—a digital sign, a full-page ad in a magazine, a billboard, and so forth.
    • Here’s one way to find a visual for this discussion: Find a bulletin board on campus. Stand across the hall from it, and identify the one piece on the bulletin board that grabs your attention.
  2. Ensure that the visual you find meets the VT Principles of Community standards.
  3. Take a photo of the visual you find, or save the visual if you found it online.
