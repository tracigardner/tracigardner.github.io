Accessibility

Paying attention to accessibility ensures that everyone can use the documents that you write. The benefit of accessibility is that an accessible document is a well-designed document. Everything you write for this course should be accessible.

Chunking

Breaking your document into small chunks makes it easier for readers. By avoiding overly long paragraphs, you can increase your document’s readability.
