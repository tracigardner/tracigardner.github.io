Chapter 8 examines how and why technical writing typically incorporates persuasive strategies. Ultimately all communication attempts to persuade the audience. Even if you only plan to share information, you have to persuade your readers that you are someone honest who is telling them the truth.

Consider the informal details that you will share on your recommendation report topic this week. Your goal is to tell me what your writing topic is, but you don’t want to just tell me—you want to persuade me that you’ve made a good choice that I should approve. Check out the “Presenting Yourself Effectively” section of Chapter 8 (starting on p. 188) for some tips that will help you make your case.
