Chapter 21 discusses effective strategies for presenting information to an audience, whether that group is colleagues and coworkers, clients, customers, or the public.

One key to effective presentations is designing slides that provide information about your topic in a clear, concise, and convincing way. The chapter specifically addresses the use of PowerPoint and other tools (beginning on page 586).

In addition to the two tutorials and the two tech tips in the textbook, the LinkedIn Learning course PowerPoint: Designing Better Slides (1h 33m) outlines three pillars that support a well-designed presentation. Skim through the titles of the individual videos in the course and watch any that will help you design your presentation.
